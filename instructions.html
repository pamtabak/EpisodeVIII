Before you can start playing, you need to click with the left button on the game screen and allow the game to remove your cursor.

To shoot, you just need to click with the mouse left button. But pay attention: you can only destroy asteroids.

To rotate the camera, just move the mouse around the screen.

To move with the spaceship:

Press W to go foward

Press S to go backward

Press A to go to the left

Press D to go to the right


There's a map of the game on the top right. It shows the game from above, so you can understant what is happening around you.

Good luck!
